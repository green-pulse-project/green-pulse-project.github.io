  • Enable global microdonations without intermediaries.

üåê Tokenomics

The total supply of GPLS is 100,000,000 tokens, distributed as follows:

  • Community (35%): Rewards, governance, and community activities.
  • Airdrop (20%): Early adoption campaigns and environmental education.
  • Development (15%): Funding for the technical team and platform improvements.
  • Green Reserve (15%): Direct funding for green initiatives.
  • Liquidity (10%): PancakeSwap and future exchange listings.
  • Marketing & Partnerships (5%): Project promotion and strategic collaborations.
